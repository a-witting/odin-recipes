Sugar Cookies

This is the best sugar cookie recipe; east to cut out and they're perfect for decorating. This is the only cookie recipe you'll need for any holiday!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1-1/2 cups unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat sugar and softened butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
